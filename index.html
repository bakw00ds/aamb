Built By A Barber. Run Like A Shop.

Joe Kline opened the doors in 2004 with a simple idea: a place men actually wanted to hang out in.

Joe's been cutting hair longer than most of his regulars have been growing it. He opened All About Men in 2004 in Frederick, Maryland, after years behind the chair figuring out what a men's shop ought to be — and what it ought not to be.

The answer was simple. Great cuts, done by people who actually like cutting hair. A cold beer if you want one. A game on the TV. Shuffleboard out back. No fuss, no attitude, no upsell. Just a proper barbershop, the way they used to be, with a few things the old ones never thought of.

— Joe

— Questions, Answered —

The Stuff People Ask Us

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?
Walk-ins are welcome up until 15 minutes before close. Appointments are recommended — especially on Saturdays — to reduce wait time. Book online through our booking system or call us at (301) 682-9992.
How long does a typical appointment last?
Most cuts run 15 to 30 minutes. A haircut plus beard trim runs 30 to 45 minutes. Specialty shaves (like our Smooth Operator bald head shave) take about 30 minutes on their own.
What payment methods do you accept?
Cash, check, and all major credit cards except American Express. We also accept Apple Pay. We do not accept Venmo.
Do you sell gift cards?
Yes — gift certificates are available in any amount. Call us at (301) 682-9992 to purchase one. They're always in stock, no waiting.
What products do you use and sell?
We use and retail Suavecito and our in-house Angry Barber line — beard balms, beard oils, and the Angry Barber Beard Line tool we use at the chair.
Is the beer real? Can I actually get a beer with my cut?
Yes, beer is complimentary with a haircut — age permitting. We've got a real in-house bar and a shuffleboard table. This is not a gimmick.
Is the shop kid-friendly?
Very. We've got a dedicated kids' section with toys and video games, and many of our barbers specialize in cutting for kids who are picky or nervous in the chair.

What To Expect On Your First Visit

If you've never been, here's the short version. No surprises. No gimmicks. Just a good cut and an even better hang.

Walk In Or Book Ahead

Walk-ins welcome up until 15 minutes before close. For Saturdays and after-work slots, book online — it's the easiest way to skip the wait.

Plan For 15–45 Minutes

A standard cut runs about 15–20 minutes. Adding a beard trim pushes to 30–45. Specialty shaves take about 30 on their own.

Payment & Parking

Cash, check, and all major cards except Amex. Apple Pay works too. Free parking in the lot. No Venmo — sorry.

Stick Around A While

Grab a complimentary beer with your cut (age permitting), catch the game on our TVs, or challenge someone to shuffleboard. The kids section has toys and video games if they're tagging along.

Accessibility

All About Men Barbershop is committed to ensuring our website and physical shop are accessible to every guest. This website aims to follow WCAG 2.2 Level AA guidelines — keyboard navigation, descriptive image text, and strong color contrast. We continuously work to improve.
